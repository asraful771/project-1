This project about links.

Link is a relationship between two things or situations.
We have three kinds of links.
1. Inpage link.
2. Internal link.
3. External link.
To demonstrate- I made a page with a menu. this menu has the top, middle, bottom. it represents for an in-page link.

For the internal link, I made another page. if you click the internal link to the menu, it will go to the internal page.

For the external link, I had a link to its Google page. if you click External link , you will go google search page.
